Product

I was on the Facebook App Monetization team

Problem to be solved

Small advertisers don't have the time or money to successfully advertise with video ads on Meta. How might we enable SMB's with tools to help them fully utilize the power of video advertising.

Scope

Sole designer responsible for creating video advertising tools (Video Creation Kit and ML assisted video editing tools).

Video Creation Kit is a tool that takes images and automatically creates a short form video.

ML assisted trimming finds the most important aspect of a long video and trims it to the recommended 6s or less format.

ML assisted cropping takes your landscape video and crops the video to a square according to where the subject is.

Contributions
